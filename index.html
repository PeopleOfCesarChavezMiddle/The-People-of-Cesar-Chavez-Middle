A glimpse into the stories of real people at Cesar Chavez Middle School in East Palo Alto, CA

What makes someone a good friend?

What makes someone a good friend is their actions, like their actions that they are going to choose to be, like they would have two choices: to be in class or ditch class. For me, I would look for to have a friend that would stay in class and like get a good education and life. And when we hang out, have fun. And not be mean. Another reason for a good friend is that they are always going to be there for you and mostly always on your side. And they would take care of you and thank you.

What is something you appreciate about your parents?

Something I appreciate about my parents is that, you know, they never let you down. They try to always support you in whatever decisions you make. My mom is usually always there for me. For the Share your Spark, she is definitely going to be there. She said she would try to make it and I know that when she says she will definitely try to make it. You know, my mom is like the greatest part of me because, you know, she has had a rough past and now, like, she is trying to do the right part in helping her children succeed. So we don't have to live through the same past that she did when she was little.

What does it mean to be a good sister to your siblings?

What it means to be a good sister to your siblings is that, you know, you have to love them cause, you know, sometimes they, no, they love you, but they cans sometimes be annoying but I still love them. To be a good sibling is to like always respect them, play with them, so they can also love you too.

What is your one of your best memories?

One of my best memories is when my uncle and aunt would live in this big house that had four rooms, a pool, a little putting station. I remember that everyday after we were done in the pool and after we would all take a shower, I would sleepover at their house and, with the youngest of my 3 cousins, we would both play around and do eachother's makeup and then we would come out while the rest of my family was watching tv in the living room. And we would come out and show them how we made eachother look and then we would go back into her room and we would do another look. And we'd just do this for hours.
